This paper proposes AOPDet, with the core contribution of proposing a non sequential corner representation method as a novel rotation target representation, designing an automatic organization mechanism to guide the model to learn target corners, and designing a dedicated detection head structure. Improved 17.0 mAP compared to baseline on the publicly available aerial dataset, reaching SOTA level.
