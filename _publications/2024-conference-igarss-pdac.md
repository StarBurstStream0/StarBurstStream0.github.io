This paper proposes a PDAC network that uses adapter fine-tuning encoders instead of training from scratch, coupling DNN and ACM with a very small number of parameters to achieve end-to-end building segmentation. On two major building datasets, the effectiveness of the parameter efficient fine-tuning method was verified by achieving better performance than the baseline with nearly half of the computing resources.
